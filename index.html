About Us

AgriSolutions.

Agri Solutions is a leading provider of innovative agricultural products and services designed to enhance farm productivity and profitability. With over 30 years of experience in the seed industry, Agri Solutions offers a wide range of products including high-quality seeds, low salt liquid fertilizers, and advanced seed treatments. Their commitment to genetic diversity and cutting-edge agricultural technology ensures that farmers receive the best possible return on investment.


In addition to their product offerings, Agri Solutions provides a suite of precision agricultural services. These services include soil sampling, yield mapping, and planting prescriptions, all aimed at optimizing crop performance and soil health. By leveraging these precision services, farmers can make data-driven decisions that improve efficiency and sustainability on their farms.


Agri Solutions operates globally, with production plants and sales offices in various regions, ensuring they can meet the needs of farmers worldwide. Their dedication to customer service and quality has made them a trusted partner in the agricultural industry.
